Assignment 4: Navbar and Dropdown Menu (15 points)
Objective: Build a responsive navigation bar with a dropdown menu.
Tasks:
1. Navbar Creation (5 points):
    Design a navbar with a brand/logo on the left and navigation links on the right.
2. Dropdown Menu (5 points):
    Add a dropdown menu to one of the links.
3. Responsive Toggle (5 points):
    Ensure the navbar collapses on smaller screens, with a toggle button for menu items.
